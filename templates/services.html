Osumi Canyoning + Lunch at Hotel Kanione

Explore Albania’s most iconic canyons with a full day of adventure! This tour is designed for adults looking to enjoy a mix of adrenaline and stunning nature in the heart of Skrapar.

The day includes hiking, swimming, and canyoning through the beautiful Osumi Canyons with professional guides, followed by a traditional Albanian lunch in a local guesthouse.

Duration: approx. 4–5 hours

Included: guide, helmet, life jacket, transport, lunch, photos

What to bring: towel, sportswear, shoes for water, dry clothes

Pirogoshi Caving Adventure + Lunch at Hotel Kanione

Discover the largest cave in Albania with this underground adventure, perfect for adults who enjoy natural wonders, stories, and light physical activity.

The tour includes guided exploration through tunnels, halls, and pools, followed by a delicious lunch in the beautiful countryside of Skrapar.

Duration: approx. 2.5–3 hours

Included: helmet with headlamp, gloves, local guide, transfer, photos, lunch

What to bring: comfortable clothes, hiking shoes, light jacket, backpack

This light hiking tour around the Pirogoshi region is perfect for those who prefer walking through forests and mountain paths rather than entering the cave itself.
