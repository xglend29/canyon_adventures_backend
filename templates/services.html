Pirogoshi Cave Adventure

Pirogoshi Cave Exploration is a fascinating underground journey through Albania’s largest cave. This tour is perfect for nature lovers, curious explorers, and those who enjoy combining a light physical challenge with incredible natural sights.

The cave features majestic rock formations, tunnels, halls, and pools carved over thousands of years. The experience is guided by professionals who share geological facts and local legends.

Duration: approx. 2.5–3 hours

Included: helmet with headlamp, gloves, local guide, transfer, photos, and free parking.

What to bring: comfortable clothing, hiking shoes, light jacket, backpack, and water.
